Our Story

Inspired by the challenges Rwandan farmers faced in accessing markets for their harvests, I envisioned an intuitive application to enhance their market presence. Recognizing their struggles, I dedicated myself to developing this solution, aiming to empower farmers and help them thrive by connecting them directly to potential buyers.

Mission

Agri-Connect empowers farmers to diversify crops and create sustainable jobs through a mobile app.

Vision

We aim to foster climate-resilient agriculture, empowering farmers and creating sustainable jobs through innovative technology.
